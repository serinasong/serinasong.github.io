Week 3 Reading Response
Acquiring abstract knowledge largely depends on an individual's level of understanding and cognition. With the rapid expansion of the internet and website development, our brains are inundated with diverse and often overwhelming information. The contemporary challenge has shifted from a scarcity of information to effectively filtering out the useful from the superfluous. The question that arises in my mind is how we distinguish between redundant and crucial information when creating our own website.
